This project conforms to level [AA WCAG 2.0](https://www.w3.org/WAI/GL/WCAG20/) standards as required by the university's [accessibility policy](https://adminguide.stanford.edu/chapters/computing/digital-accessibility/accessibility-electronic-content). For more information on the policy please visit the Admin Guide accessibility policy linked above, [Stanford's Accessibility statement](https://www.stanford.edu/site/accessibility/) and the [Stanford's Office of Digital Accessibility](https://soda.stanford.edu/).

If you find an accessibility error or have a suggestion please [let us know](mailto:decanter-discuss@lists.stanford.edu).
