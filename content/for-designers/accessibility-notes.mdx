This project conforms to level [AA WCAG 2.0](https://www.w3.org/WAI/GL/WCAG20/) standards as required by the university's [accessibility policy](https://ucomm.stanford.edu/policies/accessibility-policy.html). For more information on the policy please visit University Communications accessibility policy and the [Stanford Online Accessibility Program](https://soap.stanford.edu/).

If you have found an accessibility error or have a suggestion please create a ticket.
